Design Specifications:

The Design Specification include the following list: - A clear title of the subject of your data - A clear presentation of your data(in this case the % of each country) - A pullout bar or list of all the countries - A section for intresting facts(ex. average of Africa) - A body text with the publishers name for recognition.

If all these criterias are met then the sucess criteria would be low to average. In order to elevate the sucess of the design you can add advances Design Specification such as the following:

- A colour coded system with the colours relatng to each country - Flags beside each country to add a more advanced layout - An option to look at more than one countries data to compare the statistics - Multiple categories for statistics such as the averages of male, female and both genders - A pullout bar rank of the countries %.

By creating these advanced Design Specifications this can boost the popularity and level of your website. Popular websites use these creative features to please the user and create a more likely chance that they will visit or share the website.
